#World War II: The Clash Of Nations!

The project World War II: The Clash Of Nations! is designed to depict major battles of World War II. It uses the C language and "txt" files.

This game is a multiple-choice game that influences the outcome of the story.

#Installation
Download the folder.
Execute the "jeu_sae_gm.exe" file.

#Usage
Use the keys 1 and 2 to make your choices and navigate through different levels.
